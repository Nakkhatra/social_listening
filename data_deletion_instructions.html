Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete any data associated with your use of social_listening, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@sociallistening.app
  2. Use the subject line: Facebook Data Deletion Request
  3. Include the Facebook user ID or email used to log in

We will verify the request and delete all data associated with your Facebook account within 7 business days. Note: We do not store your data beyond your session, but this process ensures removal from logs or temporary caches.
